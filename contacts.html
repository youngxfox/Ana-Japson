About

  Ana Japson is a makeup artist with an international background. Born in Russia, Ana has traveled through Europe, Asia and America, where she spotted beauty of many different cultures, greatly influencing her vision for makeup.
She started her career in the beauty industry as a model, and soon developed a passion for makeup, so her model experience gave her understanding of photography and lighting techniques. Worked in several fields from TV to model agencies and ballet, currently for fashion designers. Her works published in Harper`s BAZAAR Czech Republic, HUF magazine and Le Fil D`or magazine.
